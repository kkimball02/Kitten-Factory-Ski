About Us

Welcome to Kitten Factory, your go-to destination for high-quality custom skis. Founded by a group of ski enthusiasts and mechanical engineers from the University of Utah, we specialize in creating the best carbon fiber skis for every terrain and skill level.

Our mission is to enhance your skiing experience with our top-of-the-line, durable, and lightweight skis. Since our inception in 2010, we have committed to pushing the boundaries of design and technology to produce skis that meet the highest standards of performance and sustainability.

At Kitten Factory, we believe in supporting our local economy and community. All our products are proudly designed and manufactured in Utah, and we are actively involved in promoting winter sports and environmental conservation in our community.
